## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Data Analyst with a background that most analysts don't have** — before working with data professionally, I spent years as a Mental Health Support Worker managing case records, tracking behavioural trends, and maintaining accuracy where the cost of error was real. That experience shaped how I approach data: with **precision, accountability, and a focus on what decisions the data actually needs to drive**.

I've since applied that mindset in data-driven roles at two of the UK's largest institutions — **HM Revenue & Customs** and the **Home Office** — working with structured financial and sensitive case-level data in regulated, high-volume environments where accuracy is non-negotiable.

I specialise in turning raw, complex data into **clear dashboards, structured reports, and actionable insight** — the kind that supports real decisions, not just fills a slide deck.

> 🎯 Seeking Data Analyst roles in **healthcare, the public sector, or any environment where data drives meaningful impact**. Open to conversations — feel free to connect.
